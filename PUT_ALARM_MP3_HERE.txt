PUT YOUR ALARM SOUND HERE
========================

File needed: alarm.mp3

Requirements:
- Format: MP3
- Duration: at least 10 seconds (it loops, so length doesn't matter much)
- Volume: loud and jarring is the point

Free sources:
- https://freesound.org (search "alarm clock")
- https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/alarm/
- Any loud ringtone MP3 renamed to alarm.mp3

After placing the file here, the app will use it as the looping alarm sound.
The notification channel in AlarmService.js also references 'alarm.mp3' for
the system-level notification sound on Android.
